Rocky Mountain Ski and Sport

Background

Rocky Mountain Ski and Sport (RMSS) is a locally owned ski and snowboard shop based in Colorado Springs, Colorado. The shop was founded with a mission to make winter sports more accessible, more sustainable, and more community centered. Instead of encouraging constant gear replacement, RMSS focuses on repairing equipment, extending product lifecycles, and promoting the use of high-quality used gear.

RMSS specializes in:

  • High-quality used skis, snowboards, boots, and bindings
  • Professional tuning and repairs to extend equipment life
  • Safe fittings for riders of all experience levels
  • Packaging online orders using reused and recycled materials
  • Providing budget-friendly gear for families, beginners, and experienced riders
  • Educating the community through in-store expertise and social media content

This combination of sustainability, affordability, and knowledge has made RMSS a trusted resource for skiers and snowboarders across the Colorado Springs and Pueblo region.

How Rocky Mountain Ski and Sport Reduces Waste in the Ski Industry

Repairing Existing Gear

RMSS performs waxes, tunes, edge sharpening, P-tex repairs, and full safety checks. These repairs keep skis and snowboards riding longer, reducing the need for new manufacturing and minimizing equipment waste.

Reselling Quality Used Equipment

Buying used gear keeps equipment out of landfills, cuts down on emissions tied to production, and makes snow sports accessible to more people. Every used item is inspected, refurbished, and tuned so customers get safe, high-quality gear at a fraction of the cost.

Reusing and Recycling Packaging

From shipping boxes to bubble wrap, RMSS prioritizes reusing and recycling all packing materials. This reduces the environmental footprint of online orders and models sustainable business practices for the ski community.
